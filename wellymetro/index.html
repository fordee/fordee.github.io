Welly Metro

Welly Metro is a simple commuter app that quickly and easily tells you when your next Wellington bus or train is due.

Add favourite stops and routes to instantly find your next departure times.

The companion Apple Watch app uses your favourite stops and routes to conveniently show your next bus or train time right on your wrist.

Privacy Policy

Welly Metro does not collect any personal or other data.

Location data

Welly Metro uses location data to show where you are on the map so you can find the closest bus or train stop. This data stays on your device and is never collected.

Metlink API

Welly Metro uses the Metlink API service to fetch bus and train times. No login or account is required, and no personal data is sent when the service is called.
